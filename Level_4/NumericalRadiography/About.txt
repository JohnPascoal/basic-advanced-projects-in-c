A C program that receives a number and tells its characteristics.

Signal:
	Positive (greater than zero)
	Negative (less than zero)
	Zero

Parity:
	Even (divisible by 2)
	Odd (not divisible by 2)

Magnitude:
	Integer
	Decimal (or floating point)

Divisibility:
	Prime (divisible only by 1 and itself)
	Composite (divisible by numbers other than 1 and itself)
